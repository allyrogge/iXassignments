What I'm All About

Learning from Those Around Us

For nearl a decade, I've volunteered with a program called BuddyUp. BuddyUp is a Cincinnati-based coalition of mentors and children with Down syndrome. This experience has enriched my life and reaffirmed my belief in the value (for both parties) of mentorship programs.

Making Music

As you can read about in my blog below, I've found great importance in music: creating art and sharing it has shaped me since age 4. From Croatia to St. Paul's Cathedral in the Vatican, I've shared this universal art form with many people and have learned about the value of music from just as many.

Exploration

Nothing is more important than the ability to know the world and those that occupy it. That can best (and perhaps, only) be achieved when we can empathsize with different types of people. From Europe to my current adventures in South Africa, I've gotten to learn about many types of people.

Family/Friends

Raised by some of the best Midwesterners around, I am a believer in the power of faith, family, and good ole' fashioned frienship. Through the tough times my family has gone through, we have drawn closer through the power of community.
